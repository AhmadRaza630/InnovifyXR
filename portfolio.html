Projects Powered by Innovation & Immersive Technology

We are proud to showcase our most impactful projects built with cutting-edge immersive technologies. From startups to global enterprises, we help businesses transform their ideas into powerful digital experiences.
Our expertise spans industries such as retail, e-commerce, healthcare, education, entertainment, real estate, gaming, and enterprise training. By blending creativity with advanced tech, we deliver solutions that streamline operations, enhance engagement, and unlock new possibilities.
Using AR/VR, AI, mobile apps, blockchain, and interactive 3D environments, we create smart, future-ready solutions that keep brands ahead in a fast-evolving digital world.

AR Restaurant Menu

A digital AR-based restaurant menu that replaces traditional paper menus. Customers can view realistic 3D food models, explore ingredients, and get an enhanced ordering experience.

AR Product Showcase

An AR tool for businesses to display their products in 3D from any angle and in real-world environments. Ideal for e-commerce, exhibitions, retail, and branding.

AR HomeVista

Allows customers to visualize furniture, décor items, wall colors, and layouts inside their homes using AR. Helps reduce returns and boosts customer confidence.

VR Trainings

A suite of VR training experiences designed to improve learning outcomes, skill development, and safety operations across multiple industries.

VR Welding Training Simulator

A realistic welding practice simulation for technical training institutes. Includes metal-cutting mechanics, real-time scoring, heat mapping, spark effects, and safety feedback.

HelixVault DeFi Platform

Decentralized finance platform development.

VR Fire Extinguisher Training Simulator

A VR-powered emergency response training tool that teaches users to operate fire extinguishers correctly in various fire scenarios with real-time feedback.
